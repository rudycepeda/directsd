Internal properties of polynomials

For a (quasi)polynomial p the following basic properties can be obtained

p.zarray of roots (zeros)
p.z(i)root # i
p.kleading coefficient
p.coefarray of coefficients
p.coef(i)coefficient # i
p.degdegree of the polynomial
p.varvariable of the polynomial
p.shiftdegree shift of the discrete-time polynomial

Example

    p = 8*poln ( [2 3 4], 'rz', 1 )
 
 p: quasipolynomial
   8 z^2 - 72 z + 208 - 192 z^-1
 
    p.z
 
 ans =
      2
      3
      4
 
    p.z(2)
 
 ans =
      3
 
    p.k
 
 ans =
      8
 
    p.coef
 
 ans =
      8   -72   208  -192
 
    p.coef(3)
 
 ans =
    208
 
    p.var
 
 ans =
 z
 
    p.shift
    
 ans =
     1
